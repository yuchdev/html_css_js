HTML5 audio

The <audio> tag is used to embed sound content in a document, such as music or other audio streams. The <audio> tag contains one or more <source> tags with different audio sources. The browser will choose the first source it supports.
There are three supported audio formats in HTML: MP3, WAV, and OGG.

HTML5 video

The <video> tag is used to embed video content in a document, such as a movie clip or other video streams. Just like <audio>, the <video> tag contains one or more <source> tags with different video sources. The browser will choose the first source it supports.
There are three supported video formats in HTML: MP4, WebM, and OGG.
